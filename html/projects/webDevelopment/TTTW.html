TTT Web

This is my Final Degree Project. It consists of the web migration of an already existing program (TaskTimeTracker), which enabled users to easily create bar graphs.

At its core, the program was intended to help math scholars study the steps that are followed by a group of students during the resolution of a problem, together with the time extension of these steps. It is based on the representation diagrams presented by Ärlebäck, and can be applied to any problem-solving process (mathematical or not) that can be divided into stages or categories over time.

In this new version, the tool is more accessible for everyone and the degree of personalization is greater. It also includes new options, such as loading audio/video (necessary for the "problem solving process" temporal analysis), adding annotations or save and load the created data and visualize it in a variety of ways (pie charts or table).
