Colegas

This was my first attempt at designing a t-shirt, something that I wanted to do for a long time.
The concept comes from the importance that, I believe, friends have in your life, and how much it improves when you are surrounded by those you love. For the logo, I wanted to make a pun with the brand "Colgate", very similar to the Spanish slang for friends, "colegas".
