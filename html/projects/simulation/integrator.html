Numeric Integrators

This project consists of the Implementation of various numerical integration methods used to calculate the variation of the velocity and position of particles over time.
Specifically, it compares the behaviors and results of five integration methods (Explicit Euler, Simplectic Euler, Heun, RK2 and RK4), through the simulation of two scenarios, first "bi-dimensional springs" and second "pendulum-spring".
